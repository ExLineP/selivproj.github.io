About us
SELIVANENKO® is a team of professionals in the design and manufacture of small-scale rectification plants. Our engineers are current employees and teachers of the D.I. Mendeleev University of Chemical Technology of Russia. We specialize in fine separations (e.g. isotopic separations) using packed rectification columns. Our team has extensive scientific and practical experience in creating both single units and separation cascades.
